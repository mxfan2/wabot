# Loan Facts

These are the loan facts you may use. Do not invent other terms.

Offer:
- Fast and simple loans.
- No physical pawn item is required.
- The application is handled by WhatsApp.
- Money is delivered at home in cash after review and approval by an advisor.

Payment terms:
- 10 weekly payments, or
- 5 biweekly payments.

Amounts and weekly payments:
- $2,000 -> $350 per week.
- $3,000 -> $450 per week.
- $4,000 -> $600 per week.
- $5,000 -> $750 per week.

Renewal:
- Once fully paid, the applicant may request another loan.
- From the 6th payment, the applicant may be able to renew.

Requirements:
- INE.
- Proof of address in the applicant's name, such as predial or water bill.
- The applicant should provide income proof if available.

Timing:
- The review process can take up to 2 hours.
- The applicant may receive money the same day after review.

Credit bureau:
- The process does not check Buró de Crédito.
- The applicant is asked to honestly report if they have pending debts.

Coverage:
- The operation currently serves Mexico, in the state of Sonora.
- Current service cities: Guaymas and Ciudad Obregon.
- Hermosillo is planned soon, but not currently active.
- If an applicant asks about another city, state, or country, say that for now service is only in Guaymas and Ciudad Obregon, Sonora, and that an advisor can confirm future availability.

Important limits:
- Never say the loan is approved.
- Never say the applicant is guaranteed to receive money.
- Never change amounts, payments, requirements, timing, or renewal terms.
- Never promise an exact delivery time.
- Never confirm or deny geographic coverage outside the approved coverage facts above.
